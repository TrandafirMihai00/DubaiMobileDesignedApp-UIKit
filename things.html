30 things to do in Dubai

The best of Dubai shore to shore

1. Dive in to the not so deep and learn to surf at Laguna Waterpark – home to an epic 180-degree surf-machine and a whole lot of other thrills in between.
2. Explore Dubai's azure blue waters by taking a fishing trip out to sea with the experts, like Deep Blue Sea Fishing.
3. Tear yourself away from our beachy shores to cross the historic Dubai Creek onboard a traditional wooden abra.
4. Gather the family for a chance to walk underwater (figuratively) at Dubai Aquarium's magical tunnel and say hi to the rays.
5. Chase the wind and go all out with jetskiing at sea – the team at Seawake are one of the best in the city.
6. Sleeping with the fishes takes on a whole new meaning at Atlantis, The Palm's luxe underwater suites.
7. Scare-seekers will want to take a 'leap of faith' at Aquaventure Waterpark and plunge through shark-filled waters.
8. Getting fit doesn't need to be boring. Slip and slide your way into fun at the AquaFun inflatable waterpark by Jumeirah Beach Residences.
9. Escape the bright lights of the city and go kayaking in a shimmering lake nestled amid the mountainscapes of Hatta.
10. Take the plunge into the world’s deepest pool and explore depths of up to 60m at Deep Dive Dubai.

11. When you'd like to combine a sea and sky experience, hop aboard a seaplane that takes off on water and then rewards you with aerial views of the city.
12. As a city with a famed coastline, there's nothing like seeing the sights from the water, especially since water taxis are an official mode of public transportation. Choose the route from Dubai Festival City Mall to Jumeirah to see what we mean.
13. Dubai is an excellent place to get a custom suit or bespoke dress. Head to the decades-old tailoring shops in the neighbourhood in Satwa for styles that are a cut above.
14. Looking for the perfect fabric? You'll find everything from soft cotton to pure silk at Textile Souk in Bur Dubai where eager owners offer bargain prices the more you order.
15. Coffee is the elixir of life for many. If you agree, you'll want to learn all about the wonders of the bean at Dubai's dedicated Coffee Museum.
16. The bright lights of the city's malls are irresistible but, if you're looking for special deals on the best designers, make time for a trip to the Dubai Outlet Mall.
17. A visit to Dubai's atmospheric Gold Souk is a must with storefronts filled with intricate, bejewelled designs that are worth every dirham.
18. Explore big name brands and indie boutiques at Souk Madinat Jumeirah – you'll find gifts galore, including custom name pendants and Aladdin-inspired lamps.
19. Find the weird and wonderful for sale in Dragon Mart, one of the largest trading centres for Chinese products outside mainland China.
20. Every serious shopper's dream come true, there's nothing quite like The Dubai Mall. With over 1,000 stores, you're bound to find it all here.

21. When you want to combine shopping with a bit of sun, head north to La Mer. This outdoor destination is a treat for all ages and presents a curated selection of boutiques.
22. Create your own bespoke scent at the Perfume Souk in Dubai and take it home in colourful glass bottles – they make great souvenirs!
23. An obvious recommendation – but one that always bears repeating – is taking in the sights from At The Top, Burj Khalifa, the tallest building in the world.
24. Whether you want to singalong with A-list stars, watch world-class theatre or catch a comedy performance like no other, there's always something fun on at the Coca-Cola Arena.
25. Play spot the turtle at the serene lagoons of Jumeirah Al Naseem hotel – the setting is unique and it's surrounded by a slew of award-winning restaurants.
26. Watch the sunrise while horse riding at Al Jiyad Stables. It's a transformative experience and, once you try it, you'll want to book your next ride immediately.
27. See the world in a stroll around Global Village. Taking place every year from October to early May, it's an amusement park like no other.
28. Take the kids to Dubai Garden Glow, a magical world where glowing sculptures astound and dinosaurs roam free.
29. Marvel at the exotic wildlife in The Green Planet's bio-dome. That's right, it's an indoor rainforest in one of the world's most exciting desert cities.
30. Walk along Dubai Water Canal. One of the greatest urban transformations in modern time, this historic waterway has effectively made parts of the city into an island.
