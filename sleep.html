Luxury hotels

Mövenpick Hotel Jumeirah Village Triangle

B1 Joory Street District 3 - Jumeirah Village Triangle - Dubai

The family-friendly Mövenpick Hotel Jumeirah Village Triangle boasts many highlights – panoramic views of the city skyline and a host of amenities to keep all ages entertained.

W Dubai - Mina Seyahi

King Salman Bin Abdulaziz Al Saud St - Dubai Marina - Dubai

The impressive views of the yacht-filled harbour and endless blue sea beyond should be reason enough to check into the W Dubai - Mina Seyahi. This chic skyscraper, which started welcoming guests in April, boasts an infinity pool hosting the Wet Deck by Ginger Moon that ups the ante on waterside revelry. There are also five irresistible dining destinations, alongside a fitness studio and spa.

Taj Exotica Resort & Spa The Palm

Plot no. PJCR39 East - Crescent Rd - Dubai

Get ready for a taste of Indian hospitality as the Taj Exotica makes its debut in Dubai this April. Expect full on glamour as the waterside resort differentiates itself in innovative new ways. See suites sporting jacuzzis, get zen at the in-house School of Yoga, or watch the sunset from the longest pool on The Palm.

Marriott Resort Palm Jumeirah

Marriott Resort Palm Jumeirah - Dubai

Situated near Palm West Beach, the Marriott Resort Palm Jumeirah offers so many facilities that your family will have plenty to experience and enjoy in the property. Expected to open this summer, the perks here include the five-star sumptuous Saray Spa, an indoor Kidz Club, eight restaurants spanning cuisines from Mexican to Korean, plus plenty of watersports and luxury pools.

INK Hotel

Building 20, Al Jadaf Waterfront - Dubai

Adding a splash of fun to Al Jaddaf Waterfront is four-star boutique hotel INK. The chic five-storey property is the latest hotel in Dubai with a sustainability focus, with a range of organic soaps, rooftop solar panels and staff uniforms made of recycled plastic among its eco-friendly highlights.

Hyatt Centric Jumeirah Dubai

La Mer Beach - Jumeirah - Jumeirah 1 - Dubai

This boutique hotel recently debuted in the Middle East with its Dubai opening. Hyatt Centric Jumeirah Dubai features vivacious dining concepts and 173 rooms fitted in a playful fusion of colours, textures and curated trinkets. Situated in La Mer, the hotel has direct access to all of the seaside destination’s many thrills, from shoreside dining to the Laguna Waterpark.

25hours Hotel Dubai One Central

Trade Center St - Trade Centre - Trade Centre 2 - Dubai

This charming boutique hotel from Europe is sweeping Dubai off its feet. 25hours' first location in the region is situated right in the heart of Dubai at One Central. Visitors can take advantage of a host of unique delights, including its famous Bavarian-style dining garden, creative co-working spaces, a bartering zone where guests can trade items for stays, in-house cinema, dedicated vinyl-playing areas and even a pottery studio.

Anantara World Islands Dubai Resort

World Island - Dubai

Anantara’s seventh UAE property is taking up residence on none other than Dubai’s World Islands, clinching the coveted title as the archipelago’s first resort. Accessible via a 15-minute boat ride from The Palm, the resort’s home island (named Clarence Island) serves as a private haven for guests, offering 70 lavish seaside villas and suites as well as pools, alfresco dining and beach access.

LEGOLAND Hotel

Dubai Parks and Resorts, Dubai World Central, Dubai

LEGO bricks are fun no matter how old you are, which makes Dubai's LEGOLAND Hotel the perfect location for a family getaway. Kids and adults can immerse themselves in a world of creative imagination while staying in one of the property's 250 themed rooms. In addition to all the interactive toy sets, there's also a castle play area and a disco elevator.

Top 3 best low price hotels

3. Leva Hotel Mazaya Centre

Mazaya Centre - Sheikh Zayed Collector Rd - Al Wasl - Dubai

Located beside the Burj Khalifa and City Walk, Leva Hotel Mazaya Centre is just a stone's throw away from some of the city's biggest attractions. The highly-rated hotel gets rave reviews about everything from the comfy beds to the artistically-designed rooms spanning studios, one-bedroom and two-bedroom apartments with great views. Every corner of the hotel has a story to tell, from hand-painted pieces by local artists to the fresh, organic bites at Baker’s Kitchen.

2. XVA Art Hotel

Al Fahidi St,Bur Dubai، Al Fahidi Neighborhood (formerly Bastakiya),Near Dubai Museum - Dubai

The XVA Art Hotel is hidden away in a maze of streets that make up Dubai’s Al Fahidi Historical Neighbourhood, one of the oldest areas in the city. This quaint budget hotel is cosy and full of traditional open-air spaces for you to wander through, with art hanging around every corner. There are just 13 rooms in the hotel, adding to its boutique charm, and each one of them has been designed and decorated by a different local artist. The hotel is also home to a popular art gallery and an award-winning cafe. It's the perfect place to stay if you want to soak up Dubai's rich culture as you’ll find plenty of galleries and museums nearby.

1. Hampton by Hilton

Al Barsha 1 Street, Al Barsha 1 Dubai

Al Seef is the modern heart of Old Dubai, connecting a new generation of travellers with an authentic taste of how the city once looked. Within the bustling district, which is filled with restaurants and attractions, the Hampton by Hilton Dubai Al Seef offers exceptional value. With a décor and vibe that’s both refreshing and playful, the 150-room property has all the essentials and more – think comfortable beds, free breakfasts, a swimming pool and fitness area to stay active all through your stay, and even a 24/7 snack station.
